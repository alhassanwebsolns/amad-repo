Description

The Dubai Fountain is a captivating attraction located at the base of the iconic Burj Khalifa in Dubai, United Arab Emirates. It is the world's largest choreographed fountain system and offers a stunning display of water, music, and lights.

The fountain features a series of synchronized water jets that shoot up to impressive heights, choreographed to a diverse selection of music ranging from classical to contemporary. The captivating water shows are accompanied by dazzling light effects, creating a mesmerizing spectacle that attracts visitors from around the world.

The Dubai Fountain performances take place throughout the day, with evening shows offering an enchanting experience as the illuminated jets dance to the rhythm of the music. The fountain spans over 900 feet in length and can shoot water as high as 500 feet in the air, creating a truly impressive visual display.

Visitors can enjoy the fountain shows from various viewing platforms surrounding the fountain lake, or even take a boat ride to get closer to the action. The shows are free to watch and provide a memorable experience for visitors of all ages.

The Dubai Fountain has become an iconic symbol of Dubai's vibrant culture and is a must-see attraction for anyone visiting the city. It perfectly combines music, water, and light in a spectacular display that leaves a lasting impression on its spectators.

Services Available

At The Dubai Fountain, you can enjoy a range of services and experiences to enhance your visit:

  • Spectacular Fountain Shows: Witness the captivating water shows, synchronized to music and lights, featuring a variety of choreographed performances that take place throughout the day.
  • Viewing Platforms: Explore the different viewing platforms surrounding the fountain lake, offering excellent vantage points to enjoy the shows from various angles.
  • Boat Rides: Take a boat ride on the fountain lake for a unique and up-close perspective of the water shows. Experience the magic of the fountain from the water as the jets dance around you.
  • Dining Options: Indulge in a memorable dining experience at one of the waterfront restaurants or cafes located near The Dubai Fountain. Enjoy a delicious meal while admiring the stunning views.
  • Shopping: Discover a selection of retail outlets and boutiques offering a range of products and souvenirs, allowing you to take home a piece of the fountain's magic.
  • Surrounding Attractions: Explore the surrounding area, which includes other notable attractions such as the Burj Khalifa, Dubai Mall, and the vibrant Downtown Dubai district.
  • Photography Opportunities: Capture breathtaking photos and videos of the fountain shows and create lasting memories of your visit.
