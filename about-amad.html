ALL YOU NEED TO KNOW ABOUT AMAD

AMAD, an acronym for "Ask Me About Dubai," is your premier virtual travel assistant. We are dedicated to delivering seamless travel information and helping you uncover the best of Dubai. With a passion for curating unforgettable experiences and a blend of advanced artificial intelligence technology and expert local knowledge, we offer customized travel recommendations and insider tips to make your trip exceptional. Designed and regularly updated by Al Hassan Web Solutions

At AMAD, we believe travel is a transformative experience that broadens horizons and enriches lives. That's why we've developed an innovative virtual travel assistant powered by ChatGPT an advanced language model developed by OpenAI. Our user-friendly platform makes it easy to find the information you need and plan your trip according to your preferences and budget.

Our team of travel experts and AI technology ensures you have access to the latest information on Dubai's top attractions, events, and hidden gems. From hotel recommendations and restaurant reservations to tour bookings and transportation options, we're here to provide exceptional customer service and support throughout your journey. Let us be your trusted companion on your Dubai adventure.

Whether you're visiting Dubai for the first time or you're a seasoned traveler, AMAD is your ultimate virtual travel assistant. Join us and discover Dubai like a local!

Our Mission & Vison

Mission: Our mission is to make travel planning simple, fun, and stress-free. We know that planning a trip can be overwhelming and time-consuming, especially when you're not familiar with your destination. That's why we've designed our virtual travel assistant to do the heavy lifting for you. With AMAD, you can get expert travel advice, insider tips, and customized recommendations tailored to your interests and budget.

Vision: Our vision is to become the go-to resource for anyone who wants to explore Dubai with confidence and ease. We are committed to providing personalized, reliable, and up-to-date recommendations that match our users' preferences and needs. Our ultimate goal is to help our users create unforgettable memories and achieve their travel dreams.
