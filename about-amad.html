ALL YOU NEED TO KNOW ABOUT AMAD

AMAD, an acronym for "Ask Me About Dubai," is an online travel information website that offers a comprehensive guide to Dubai's top hotels and attractions. Designed and regularly updated by Al Hassan Web Solutions, our website features photos and informative articles that provide you with deep insights into different travel destinations in Dubai, empowering you to make informed decisions about where to stay and what to see. Our team conducts extensive research to ensure that we provide you with the latest and most accurate information about Dubai's travel destinations.

We understand that finding trustworthy travel information can be challenging, as many websites offer biased or outdated content. At AMAD, we're committed to changing that. Our team is passionate about delivering the latest and most accurate recommendations for all your travel needs, whether you're seeking a luxury retreat or an action-packed adventure.

Our website features a wide range of travel information, including flights, hotels, adventure activities, and record-breaking attractions. Through our partnership with TripAdvisor, the world's leading online travel information and booking website, we provide you with the best available deals and offers, ensuring maximum value for your money.

At AMAD, we believe that travel information should be accessible and enjoyable for everyone. That's why we've created a seamless and hassle-free travel search experience that puts you in control of your journey.

Thank you for choosing AMAD, "Dubai's most trusted travel information source."

Our Mission & Vison
